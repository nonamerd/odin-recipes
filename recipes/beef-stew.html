'Slow cooker beef stew'

RECIPE

Ingredients
  • Beef Chuck Roast, cut into 2-inch chunks, excess fat removed Kosher Salt or sea salt, adjust to taste
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Gluten-free All-Purpose Flour, or Whole Wheat for non-gluten, any flour works.
  • Olive Oil: I also like using avocado oil
  • Yellow Onion, Chopped Large
  • Garlic Cloves, minced: donâ€™t try using garlic powder. Fresh garlic adds so much more flavor.
  • Carrot, or two small-medium, cut into 2-inch chunks
  • Yukon Gold Potatoes, diced into 2-inch pieces
  • Beef Broth: I like beef broth over chicken for this recipe.
  • Tomato Paste
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Bay Leaves
  • Fresh Thyme Leaves, to garnish
STEPS to create a warm beef stew.
  1. Turn on the crockpot or slow cooker
  2. Add the beef, veggies, broth, spices, and all the ingredients in together at once.
  3. Give it a nice mix
  4. Cook on low for 9-10 hours, medium 7-8 hours, and high 6-7 hours.
  5. To thicken the stew, 30 minutes before serving, remove about 1/2 cup of the liquid/sauce.
  6. Mix it with about 2 teaspoons to 1 tablespoon of cornstarch.
  7. Pour it back in and allow to cook and thicken up. Remove bay leaf, serve and enjoy.
